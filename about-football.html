All About Football

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is a popular team sport played worldwide. The objective of the game is to score more goals than the opposing team by getting a ball into their net using any part of the body except the hands and arms (except for the goalkeeper). The game is played by two teams of 11 players each, and it is widely considered the most popular sport in the world.

Football is not only about skill, but teamwork, strategy, and physical endurance play significant roles in winning matches. It's played at various levels, from local clubs to international tournaments such as the FIFA World Cup.
